Exam Instructions

Please Read the Following Instructions Carefully

  • Reporting Time: Candidates must report to the exam center at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start of the examination.
  • Identity Verification: Carry your admit card, a valid photo ID proof, and any other required documents for verification purposes. .
  • The total duration of exam is 1 hour.
  • Prohibited Items: The following items are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall:

    • - Mobile phones, smartwatches, calculators, pagers, or any other electronic devices.

    - Stationery items such as books, written notes, pens, and paper (except as permitted by the authorities).

    - Wallets, bags, sunglasses, belts, and caps.

  • Permissible Items: Candidates are allowed to carry only a transparent ballpoint pen, their admit card, and photo ID.
  • Exam Timings: The examination will be held from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM (for morning shift) or 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM (for afternoon shift). No candidate will be allowed entry after the exam starts.
  • Seating Arrangement: Candidates must sit in their allocated seats according to the seating plan displayed at the center.
  • Leaving the Exam Hall: Candidates are not permitted to leave the examination hall before the test concludes.
